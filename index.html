About my City

Nashik, situated in the northern part of Maharashtra, India, is a city rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Located approximately 165 km northeast of Mumbai and 210 km north of Pune, Nashik is often referred to as the "Wine Capital of India" due to its burgeoning wine industry.

Tourist Spots in Nashik

  • Shree Kalaram Mandir
  • Pandav Leni
  • Anjaneri Fort
  • Sita Gufa
  • Trimbakeshwar Jyotirling Temple
